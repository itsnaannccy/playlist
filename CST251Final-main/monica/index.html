The Sounds

The genres that I hold close to my music preference are: Electric Dance Music, also known as EDM, because of their bassdrops and catchy lyrics, every song is a treat. Then we have my favorite artists that are usually in the Pop Genre as well as Rap/Hip-Hop. For some chill days, I resort to Neo-Folk and Rhythm and Blues to reflect and stay calm in a situation or just to ease my mind!
