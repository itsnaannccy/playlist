Joshue's Pandemic Playlist

Though COVID-19 had been affecting the world since the start of 2020, it only truly affected my life in late March when our county shut down. Living only with my roomate suddenly my home was my world. At the start of the new I had begun a new relationship as well so with the shutting down of everything was the start of something new for me. This reflected in in a lot of my music choices that got me through the pandemic. These are my top 10 songs, full of dance beats, love lyrics, and positive lyrics:


Energy by Disclosure
just like magic by Ariana Grande
Not my Boss! by Blue Hawaii
claws by Charli XCX
Ungodly Hour by Chloe x Halle
Gimme Love by Joji
IDORU by Grimes
Rain on Me by Lady Gaga (feat. Ariana Grande)
Give Me Another Chance by Empress Of
visions by Charli XCX


The Sounds

The artists mostly fall into the Dance and Pop category. I grew up with DJ's in my family and so dance music was definitely part of my upbringing. This music, consisting of genres like Pop, Dream Pop, Electronic, Experimental, and House mixed with heartfelt loving lyrics has been a good outlet for me to take a break from the world and just vibe.
